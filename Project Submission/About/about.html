About Section

The Chunky Monkey was established in 2006 by the prodigious Gilbert Gallyson. Gilbert honed his culinary talents across a plethora of the Nation's fine dining establishments.

He relocated to Chicago in 2000, taking over the running of his family's beef sandwich store. Gilbert transformed the fortunes of this corner-bistro, introducing many of his iconic recipes such as his 'Walnut Wagu' and the esteemed 'Banana Broth'.

Gilbert then settled in Chimp City, opening the much adorned Chunky Monkey Cafe. Today, this establishment is loved across the city for its variety of jungle inspired dishes.

The premises on 1 Monkey Plaza has recently undergone extensive refurbishments as well as an inspired menu rejig.

Barrista extraordinaire Billy Collison joined up with Chunky Monkey in late 2015, bringing with him extensive experience in roasting and bean procurement.

It is our utmost wish that every customer stepping in the doors of Chunky Monkey is left with a lasting impression of culinary delight.

Our staff team of 40 work relentlessly to provide the gold standard in hospitality and customer experience.

Feel free to pop in at any time to taste our delicous takeaway coffee.

And if you fancy getting a 'taste of the jungle' with our new seasonal menu, please make sure to request a booking on our reservations page !
